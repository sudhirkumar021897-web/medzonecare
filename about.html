About Medzone Health Care Advisors

Medzone Health Care Advisors is a professionally managed healthcare consultancy firm based in Zirakpur, Punjab, delivering structured solutions in NABH accreditation, hospital compliance, empanelment, and healthcare IT systems.

Established on 01 November 2021, Medzone operates as a registered Micro Enterprise under MSME (Udyam Registration No: UDYAM-PB-20-0021484). The organization specializes in management consultancy activities with a strong focus on healthcare quality, regulatory compliance, and operational excellence.

With a commitment to excellence, Medzone has been successfully guiding healthcare institutions across India in achieving accreditation readiness, statutory approvals, and process optimization.
